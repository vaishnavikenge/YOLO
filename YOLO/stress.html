Dear stress, let's break up

Accept that there are events that you cannot control

Acceptance is not the same as resignation or passivity. We can continue to push forward despite accepting that there are things beyond our control. Here are a few tips to living with greater acceptance so you can have more joy and peace of mind.

Exercise

People who exercise also tend to feel less anxious and more positive about themselves. When your body feels good, your mind often follows.

Relaxation Techniques

Yoga. This is a form of exercise, but it can also be a meditation. There are many types of yoga. The ones that focus on slow movement, stretching, and deep breathing are best for lowering your anxiety and stress.

Laugh therapy

When you laugh, you take in more oxygen. Your heart, lungs, and muscles get a boost and your body releases those feel-good hormones. Laughter also improves your immune system, lessens pain, and improves your mood for long periods of time.

Behavior

How you respond to people directly impacts your stress levels. Manage your response with these tips:
1. Try not to overcommit yourself
2. Share the responsibility
3. Count to 10 before you respond
4. Walk away from a heated situation
