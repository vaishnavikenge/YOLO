    Role Of Good Sleep In Maintaining Mental Health

    Good sleep is essential for maintaining optimal mental health. It allows our brain to consolidate memories, process emotions, and rejuvenate neural pathways crucial for cognitive function. Adequate sleep duration and quality promote emotional resilience, reduce the risk of mood disorders like depression and anxiety, and enhance our ability to manage stress effectively. Moreover, restful sleep supports sustained focus, sharper decision-making, and boosts creativity, facilitating better problem-solving skills and learning retention.
